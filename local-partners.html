The California and Hawaiian Sugar Company was founded in 1906 and operated from 1921 to 1993 as an agricultural cooperative marketing association owned by the member sugar companies in Hawaii.

Its headquarters are in Crockett under the Carquinez Bridge in unincorporated Contra Costa County, California.
